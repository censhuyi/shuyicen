Mushroom Festival UX/UI Case Study

Timeline: Fall - Winter 2022
Deliverables: Redesign the website
Team: Self-directed with feedback from mentor and peers
Role: UX/UI Designer
Tools: Figma

Background:

Mushroom Festival located at Kennett Square, PA is a local community non-profit organization. It is a 2 days weekend event for family and friends every year in September. People are allowed to get more information about the event schedule, donations, contests, and vendors through the mushroom festival website. Their main advertising platform is Facebook where they have all posts to get people to explore the events.

“Over the past 20 years, the Mushroom Festival has donated more than $1 million in funds to local organizations for things like books, playground equipment, food, transportation, and healthcare services.”

Objectives:

• Design a new and easy-to-use website.
• Make sure the website is accessible to all users.

The Challenge

My goal for the project was to design a website with a strong foundation that helps users easily explore all functions on the website.

My high-level goals were to:
• Create a platform for deeper engagement and innovation.
• Give users more guidelines to explore the website.
• Make it easy to use for everyone.

User Persona & Research

I’ve been to the mushroom festival events in September 2022 and interviewed three users who also attended the event. I used these interviews to build the user persona. After the interview, I summarized the user research and created a user persona to represent all of the website’s users.
